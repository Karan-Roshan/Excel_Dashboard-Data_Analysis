# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project turns Vrinda Store's order data into a business-ready sales dashboard. Using Microsoft Excel, the analysis brings together sales, order volume, customer demographics, fulfilment status, geographic performance, product categories, and sales channels—so decision-makers can move from raw transactions to practical growth actions.

## The Business Story
At the end of 2022, Vrinda Store had a full year of orders but no single view of the customers behind them. Which customers were driving revenue? Which marketplaces were worth prioritising? Where was demand strongest? And, most importantly, where should the store focus to grow in 2023?

This analysis answers those questions. The dashboard follows the customer journey from an order being placed through to its delivery, then connects those orders to the people, channels, and states that generated the most value. Instead of reviewing thousands of rows one at a time, Vrinda Store can now use one interactive report to identify its most valuable audience and make smarter sales and marketing decisions.

## Objective
Create an annual sales report for 2022 that helps Vrinda Store understand customer behaviour and identify opportunities to increase sales in 2023.

## Questions Answered
- How do monthly sales and order volume compare?
- Which month recorded the highest sales and number of orders?
- Did men or women make more purchases?
- What was the distribution of delivered, cancelled, returned, and refunded orders?
- Which states contributed the most sales?
- How do age group and gender relate to order volume?
- Which sales channels generated the most orders?
- Which product categories performed best?

## Dashboard Highlights
The Excel dashboard presents the analysis through six key views:
| Area | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| Orders vs. Sales | Monthly comparison of order count and sales amount |
| Order Status | Delivered, cancelled, returned, and refunded order mix |
| Sales Channels | Order contribution by Ajio, Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra, Nalli, and others |
| Age vs. Gender | Order share across adult, senior, and teenager customer groups |
| Men vs. Women | Revenue contribution by gender |
| Top States | Leading states by sales value |

## Key Insights
- **Women were the primary customer segment**, contributing **64%** of sales, compared with **36%** from men.
- **Adult women were the strongest audience**, accounting for the largest share of orders among the age-and-gender segments shown.
- **Order fulfilment was strong**: **92.3%** of orders were delivered, while cancellations, returns, and refunds formed a comparatively small share.
- **Amazon was the leading channel** at roughly **35%** of orders. Myntra (about **23%**) and Flipkart (about **21%**) were the next most important channels.
- Sales were concentrated in a few states: **Maharashtra** led with **₹2.99M**, followed by **Karnataka** (**₹2.65M**) and **Uttar Pradesh** (**₹2.10M**). Telangana (**₹1.71M**) and Tamil Nadu (**₹1.68M**) completed the top five.
- The strongest months in the displayed dashboard were **April and May**, with May showing the highest order volume and sales value among the months shown.

## Recommendation for 2023
Focus promotions, personalised messaging, and inventory planning on **adult women in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**. Prioritise high-performing marketplaces—especially **Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart**—while maintaining the delivery performance that supports customer confidence and repeat purchases.
